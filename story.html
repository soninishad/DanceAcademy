Our Story

The year was 2001. Two college students came home to........................to the decline of proper
music education within the community.In the band room of ...................., these two students
came together to form small community classes for youth all around DanceStudio to learn music.
Impressed by the youths’ commitment and dedication to music education, on ..................,
TheDanceStudio was born.

Forging past its humble beginnings, The DanceStudio has established itself as the premier youth
marching arts organization in DanceStudio. “Who leads the way? Elite leads the way!”-an unofficial mantra
of the band- Elite is known for their unique and entertaining shows that consistently bring audiences to
their feet.

Today the program is located in East DanceStudio but is open to members in DC, Maryland, Virginia, and
Delaware. Their partnerships include the State’s Attorney’s Office for Baltimore City, Baltimore City
Mayor’s Office of Employment and Development, myDanceStudio Department of Housing and Community
Development.
