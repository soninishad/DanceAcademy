Our Mission

Who We Are: The Marching Elite was established exclusively for the charitable and educational purposes of
strengthening the under resourced communities in the state of Maryland using music and arts as a vehicle to
create positive change in the communities and the lives of the participants of the program.

Our Mission : To permanently enrich the lives of young people through a commitment to youth development
and music and arts education while providing enjoyment for our local community and audiences worldwide.



Our Vision : To create a thriving marching arts community fostering educational, interpersonal,
and emotional growth for youth in Maryland.

Our Goals : 1. Advancing and improving music and arts education in the communities we serve.
2. Advancing and improving the education of young people.
3. Promoting and protecting the social well-being of young people.
4. Encouraging physical fitness and participation in competitive sports for young people.
